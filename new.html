Learn how car safety features protect you from accidents and make driving safer. Interact with simulations to understand their impact.

Traditional Safety Features

Essential car safety technologies that have saved lives for decades.

Seatbelts

Help keep passengers securely in place during collisions, reducing injury risks.

Toggle to fasten or unfasten the seatbelt simulation.

Airbags

Deploy quickly in a crash to cushion impact and protect passengers from serious injuries.

Toggle to deploy or reset the airbag simulation.

Modern Safety Features

Advanced technologies enhancing driving safety with smart assistance and controls.

Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)

Prevents wheels from locking, helping maintain steering control during hard braking.

Toggle to activate or deactivate the ABS simulation with pulsing wheels.

Lane Assist

Warns or assists drivers to stay in their lane, preventing accidental lane departures.

Toggle to activate or deactivate lane assist simulation.

About This Project

This application educates users on essential and modern car safety mechanisms, demonstrating how they protect occupants during accidents. The interactive simulations make complex safety technologies easy to understand and highlight the importance of driving safely every day.
